Pray

Carry your leaders before God.

  • Pray through your roster one tap at a time
  • A–Z rail jumps to any leader instantly
  • Mark a prayer answered → save it as a note on their profile
  • Every prayer session is logged — no one slips through
  • Call or text a leader right from the prayer screen

Encourage

Make every leader feel seen.

  • Call, text, or email from any profile in one tap
  • Send messages with push notifications — instantly
  • Message an entire circle's roster in one click
  • Birthday list so you never miss a moment
  • Follow-up tracking flags who's overdue for a touch

Coach

Develop leaders on purpose.

  • Scorecards track each leader's growth over time
  • AI meeting prep before every one-on-one
  • Notes with dictation & AI summaries — synced to CCB
  • Circle visits & live attendance trends from CCB
  • Tracker shows who's reporting & who's slipping
  • Your Today page surfaces the Big 3 that need you now
