# Circle Summary vs. CCB LEAD App

**Scope:** Circle / Group functions only — how Valley Creek actually uses the tools.
**Prepared:** June 3, 2026

**Legend:** ✅ Matches our scope (built or intentionally excluded)

## Group functions: CCB LEAD → Circle Summary

| Group function | Status | Detail |
|---|---|---|
| **Take attendance for a meeting** | ✅ | Summary form "Who came?" — roster checkboxes, select-all, add CCB people or manual guests; writes back to CCB. |
| **See the group's meetings** | ✅ | Events tab lists recent meetings to summarize. |
| **View the group roster** | ✅ | Roster tab — every member with phone, email, birthday, last-attended. |
| **Add a member to the group** | ✅ | Search CCB + add, or "request to add" someone not yet in the system. |
| **Remove a member from the group** | ✅ | Edit-roster mode; removes from the Circle only, profile preserved. |
| **Edit a member's profile/contact info** | ✅ | **By design** — Circle Leaders aren't allowed to edit member info. |
| **Contact a group member (call/text/email)** | ✅ | Tap-to-call, text, and mailto from roster and absent-member cards. |
| **Message the whole group at once** | ✅ | **By design** — leaders don't message through the app. |
| **Member birthdays** | ✅ | Upcoming-birthday alerts (next 14 days), dismissible. |
| **Group meeting notes / summary** | ✅ | Richer than LEAD: topic, notes, prayer requests, did-not-meet reasons, custom questions. |
| **Update group details (day/time/location)** | ✅ | Built into the summary form as a change request to the Circle team. |

## Group-relevant strengths over LEAD

- **Absent-member alerts** — flags anyone unseen 15+ days, with snooze / snooze-all.
- **"Did Not Meet" tracking** with reasons (holiday, leader away, weather…).
- **Prayer requests** captured per meeting.
- **Dynamic custom questions**, conditional on met / did-not-meet.
- **Draft autosave** on the summary form.

## Bottom line

Circle Summary covers **every Circle / Group function Valley Creek uses** from the LEAD app — and adds several the LEAD app doesn't have.
